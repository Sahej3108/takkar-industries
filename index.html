About TPL

Service through quality and innovation.

TPL is a professionally managed firm driven by the principle of "Service through Quality and Innovation." We use the latest technologies in machinery, R&D and materials, along with a wealth of talent and experience, to stay ahead of the competition. Customer satisfaction sits at the core of our corporate policy, and our management and employees are committed to it every day.

Our Products

Two product families, built for demanding applications.

RoHS & REACH compliant PVC Compounds

We have the right equipment and a well-equipped R&D facility to deliver excellent products, and we undertake development of tailor-made compositions. We specialise in hi-tech compounds like UL 80 & 105°C, heat resistant and low temperature compounds down to -40°C, FRLS, and anti-rodent & anti-termite compounds, conforming to world standards such as ISI, BS and UL.

  • ZHFR / LSZH Compounds
  • UL 85 & 105°C Compounds
  • RoHS 2.0 & 3.0 Compliant PVC Compounds
  • HDPE Compounds
  • HR, HRFR, AV / AVS / AVSS, FLRY-B PVC Compound
  • FR, FRLS, Type A, B, C PVC Compound

RoHS & REACH compliant Master Batches

We offer a wide range of PVC Master Batches for wire and cable, along with Colour, White, Black and Filler Master Batches for XLPE and cable extrusion, pipe extrusion, injection moulding, blow moulding and multi-layer films. These master batches deliver good light fastness, high temperature stability, low dosage rates (LDR), excellent dispersion and superior impact strength.

  • REACH & RoHS 2.0 & 3.0 Master Batches
  • Zero Halogen Master Batches
  • Black & White Master Batches
  • Color Master Batches
  • XLPE Master Batches
  • Universal Master Batches
